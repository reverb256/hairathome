## Treatment Options Explained

### Deep Conditioning ($15)
Perfect for dry, thirsty hair that needs moisture and shine.

**What it does:**
- Restores moisture to dry, brittle hair
- Adds shine and softness
- Helps prevent breakage
- Makes hair more manageable

**Great for:**
- Color-treated hair
- Heat-damaged hair
- Naturally dry hair
- Winter hair care

**How it works:** After your wash, I apply a professional deep conditioner and let it penetrate for 15-20 minutes while you relax. Then rinse thoroughly for soft, hydrated results.

### Scalp Treatment ($20)
Revitalizing treatment for a healthy scalp foundation.

**What it does:**
- Removes product buildup
- Exfoliates dead skin cells
- Stimulates circulation
- Promotes healthy hair growth

**Great for:**
- Dry, itchy scalp
- Dandruff concerns
- Oily scalp
- Anyone wanting healthier hair from the roots

**How it works:** Gentle exfoliation followed by a soothing scalp massage with nourishing oils. Stimulates blood flow to hair follicles for healthier growth.

### Repair Treatment ($25)
Intensive treatment for damaged, over-processed hair.

**What it does:**
- Strengthens weakened hair
- Repairs damage from color and heat
- Reduces breakage
- Restores elasticity

**Great for:**
- Bleached or highlighted hair
- Frequently heat-styled hair
- Chemically treated hair
- Sun-damaged hair

**How it works:** Protein-rich formula bonds to damaged areas, filling in gaps and strengthening hair from within. Best when done regularly for cumulative benefits.
