## Your Haircut Experience

When you book a mobile haircut with Hair@Home, here's what to expect:

### Before Your Appointment
- Wear comfortable clothing
- Come with clean, dry hair (or I can wash for you)
- Have a chair available in a well-lit area
- Think about the style you want - I'm happy to consult!

### During Your Visit
I arrive fully equipped with:
- Professional styling chair
- Sharp, sanitized scissors and clippers
- Quality shampoo and styling products
- Clean capes and towels
- Portable lighting if needed

Your appointment includes:
1. **Consultation** (5-10 minutes) - We discuss your desired style, face shape, hair type, and maintenance preferences
2. **Preparation** - Cape application, hair analysis, and sectioning
3. **The Cut** - Professional cutting technique tailored to your hair type
4. **Wash & Style** - Thorough wash (if desired) and professional styling
5. **Finishing** - Detail work, neck trim, and final styling

### After Your Appointment
- Styling tips for maintaining your look at home
- Recommended products (no pressure - simple suggestions)
- Information about when to schedule your next cut

**How long does a haircut take?**
Most haircuts take 30-45 minutes from start to finish.

Yes, a wash is included. However, if you prefer a dry cut or have recently washed your hair, that's fine too.

**What if I don't know what style I want?**
No problem! During the consultation, I'll help you find a style that works with your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

**Do you cut men's hair?**
Yes! I cut all hair types and have experience with both traditional and modern men's styles.

**What's the minimum age for children's cuts?**
I work with children aged 3 and up. For very young children, a parent's lap may be used for comfort and safety.
