## Classic Bob Transformation

A sophisticated bob transformation that brings out natural texture while maintaining a polished, professional look. Perfect for the modern Winnipeg professional who wants effortless style with minimal maintenance.

## The Look

**Cut**: Modern bob with subtle face-framing layers

**Length**: Hits at the jawline - the most flattering length for most face shapes

**Styling**: Sleek and smooth with a slight bend at the ends for softness

**Finish**: Professional blowout with shine-enhancing products

## Services Performed

- **Consultation**: Discussed lifestyle, styling routine, and maintenance preferences
- **Precision Cutting**: Graduated bob technique for structure and shape
- **Texturizing**: Subtle layers for movement and softness
- **Wash & Condition**: Professional products tailored to hair type
- **Blowout**: Round brush technique for smooth, polished finish
- **Styling Tips**: Demonstration of at-home styling techniques

## Time & Details

**Total Service Time**: 45 minutes

**Hair Type**: Medium-thickness, straight to slightly wavy

**Products Used**:
- Smoothing shampoo and conditioner
- Heat protectant spray
- Round brush for volume and shape
- Lightweight styling cream
- Shine serum for finishing

## Perfect For

This bob is ideal for:
- Busy professionals who want a polished look with minimal effort
- Anyone transitioning from long to short hair
- Women seeking a low-maintenance but stylish cut
- All face shapes (jawline length is universally flattering)

## Styling Options

**At Home:**
- **Sleek & Straight**: Quick blowout with flat iron finish
- **Soft Waves**: Bend ends with a curling iron for casual days
- **Air Dry**: Apply mousse and let dry naturally for texture
- **Tuck Behind Ears**: Casual, effortless look

## Why Bobs Work

**Timeless**: Never goes out of style - a classic for good reason

**Versatile**: Works for any occasion from office to evening

**Low-Maintenance**: Looks great with minimal styling effort

**Hair-Healthy**: Regular trims keep ends fresh and prevent damage

## Maintenance Schedule

**Trims**: Every 6-8 weeks to maintain shape

**At-Home Care**:
- Professional quality shampoo and conditioner
- Regular deep conditioning (every 2 weeks)
- Heat protectant when styling
- Silk pillowcase to prevent friction while sleeping

## Bob Variations

This classic bob can be customized:
- **A-Line**: Shorter in back, longer in front
- **With Bangs**: Add fringe for different look
- **Layered**: More movement and texture
- **One-Length**: Sleek and sophisticated
- **Asymmetrical**: Modern, edgy variation

## Client Story

This client was ready for a change after years of long hair. She wanted something sophisticated but easy to style for her busy professional life. We discussed various options before settling on a classic bob.

The transformation was dramatic - she looked years younger and felt lighter and freer. The bob style makes her fine hair appear thicker and gives her a polished, professional appearance perfect for her office job.

She loves how quickly she can get ready in the morning while still looking put-together.
