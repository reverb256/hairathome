## Natural Gray Blending

Beautiful gray blending that maintains the natural beauty of silver hairs while providing dimension and preventing the harsh line of demarcation. A sophisticated approach to mature hair care that embraces natural beauty while offering polish and refinement.

## The Look

**Technique**: Demi-permanent color blending for natural results

**Result**: Soft, dimensional color that incorporates gray rather than completely hiding it

**Finish**: Shiny, healthy-looking hair with movement and depth

**Philosophy**: Enhanced natural beauty, not artificial coverage

## Services Performed

- **Consultation**: Discussed comfort level with gray and desired outcome
- **Color Formulation**: Custom demi-permanent color matching natural base
- **Application**: Strategic placement to blend grays seamlessly
- **Processing**: Gentle development that enhances shine
- **Conditioning Treatment**: Restored moisture and manageability
- **Styling**: Professional blowout to showcase the dimensional color

## Time & Details

**Total Service Time**: 75 minutes

**Starting Point**: 40% gray at temples and throughout

**Products Used**:
- Demi-permanent color (no ammonia)
- Tone-on-tone formulation
- Gloss-enhancing conditioner
- Volumizing styling products

## Perfect For

Gray blending is ideal for:
- Anyone transitioning to natural gray
- Clients who want low-maintenance color
- Men and women wanting to soften the appearance of gray
- Those seeking a sophisticated, age-celebrating look

## Gray Blending vs. Full Coverage

**Gray Blending**:
- Softer, more natural grow-out
- No harsh demarcation line
- Maintains hair's character
- Less frequent touch-ups needed
- Healthier for hair

**Full Coverage**:
- Complete color transformation
- More dramatic change
- Requires regular maintenance
- Harsher grow-out period

## The Blending Process

**Assessment**: Evaluating percentage and pattern of gray hair

**Formulation**: Matching natural base color with demi-permanent color

**Application Strategy**:
- Focus on areas with most gray
- Leave some silver peeking through for dimension
- Create natural-looking results

**Processing**: Gentle development (20 minutes)

## Why Gray Blending Works

**Natural Appearance**: No obvious "dyed" look

**Graceful Grow-Out**: No harsh line between colored and natural

**Hair Health**: Demi-permanent is gentler than permanent color

**Confidence**: Feel polished while embracing natural beauty
