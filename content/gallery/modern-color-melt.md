## Modern Color Melt

A contemporary color melt technique creating seamless transitions between complementary tones. This low-maintenance look grows out beautifully while maintaining depth and dimension throughout the hair.

## The Look

**Technique**: Color melt - gradual transition between shades

**Color Story**: Dark roots melting into caramel and golden ends

**Finish**: Glossy, multidimensional with incredible shine

**Effect**: Natural, lived-in color that appears to have developed naturally

## Services Performed

- **Color Consultation**: Selected complementary shades for melt effect
- **Base Color**: Applied darker brunette at roots
- **Mid-Tone Color**: Transitional caramel through mid-lengths
- **End Color**: Golden blonde on ends
- **Blending**: Careful application where colors meet for seamless transition
- **Gloss Treatment**: Sealed cuticle and added incredible shine
- **Styling**: Voluminous waves to showcase color dimension

## Color Melt vs. Other Techniques

**Color Melt**:
- Seamless gradient between colors
- No distinct lines of demarcation
- Very low maintenance
- Grows out beautifully
- Natural, sun-kissed appearance

**Balayage**:
- Hand-painted highlights
- More contrast between colors
- Slightly more visible grow-out

**Ombre**:
- Distinct transition line
- More dramatic contrast
- Higher maintenance

## Why Color Melt Works

**Grow-Out Grace**: No harsh line as color grows out

**Versatile**: Looks great styled straight, wavy, or curly

**Dimension**: Multiple tones create depth and movement

**Low Maintenance**: Can go 12-16 weeks between touch-ups

**Customizable**: Can be done with any color combination
