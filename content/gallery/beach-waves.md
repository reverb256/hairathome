## Romantic Beach Waves

Effortless beach waves that create natural volume and movement. This style works beautifully for both casual outings and special occasions, providing that perfect "just stepped off the beach" look.

## The Look

**Style**: Loose, flowing waves with natural movement

**Texture**: Piecey, undone finish (not too perfect)

**Volume**: Fullness at the roots and throughout

**Vibe**: Casual, romantic, and effortlessly chic

## Services Performed

- **Preparation**: Volumizing wash and lightweight conditioner
- **Root Lift**: Mousse applied at roots for volume
- **Wave Creation**: Large barrel curling iron for loose waves
- **Cooling**: Pinned and cooled to set the wave pattern
- **Breaking**: Texturizing spray to break up curls into waves
- **Finishing**: Flexible hold hairspray for movement

## Time & Details

**Total Service Time**: 45 minutes

**Hair Length**: Medium-long

**Products Used**:
- Volumizing shampoo and conditioner
- Root-lifting mousse
- 1.5" curling iron
- Texture spray for piecey finish
- Flexible-hold hairspray

## Perfect For

Beach waves are ideal for:
- Bridesmaids and wedding guests
- Date nights and special occasions
- Anyone wanting a romantic, feminine style
- Clients seeking an "undone" but polished look
- All hair types (with technique adjustments)

## Beach Waves vs. Other Styles

**Beach Waves**: Looser, more casual, less uniform than traditional curls

**Hollywood Curls**: More uniform, glamourous, structured

**Mermaid Waves**: Larger, more flowing, very romantic

**Textured Waves**: Piecey, modern, edgier finish

## Creating the Look

**For Fine Hair**:
- Focus on root volume
- Use lightweight products
- Smaller barrel for definition
- Less is more with product

**For Thick Hair**:
- Larger barrel for looser waves
- More sections for control
- Stronger hold products
- Focus on mid-lengths and ends

**For Short Hair**:
- Smaller iron (1" or less)
- Focus on ends and texture
- Piecey application
- Less uniform waves

## At-Home Maintenance

**Next-Day Refresh**:
- Dry shampoo at roots
- Scrunch with water spray
- Touch up with iron on face-framing pieces
- Reapply texture spray

**Sleep Protection**:
- Loose bun high on head
- Silk or satin pillowcase
- Pineapple method for longer hair
- Don't sleep with wet hair!

**Between Washes**:
- This style often looks better on day 2-3
- Refresh rather than restyle
- Add volume with dry shampoo
- Embrace the messy look!

## Why Beach Waves Work

## Styling Variations

**Side-Swept**: All waves swept to one side for romance

**Half-Up**: Casual variation that keeps hair off face

**Accessorized**: Add flowers, pins, or headbands

**Messy**: Emphasize the undone texture

**Polished**: More uniform waves for formal occasions

## Product Recommendations

For maintaining beach waves at home:
- **Texturizing Spray**: Essential for piecey finish
- **Sea Salt Spray**: Adds grit and texture
- **Dry Shampoo**: Adds volume and absorbs oil
- **Lightweight Oil**: Tames frizz without weighing down
- **Flexible-Hold Hairspray**: Movement without stiffness
