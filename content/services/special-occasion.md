## Your Special Occasion Experience

### The Process

**For Weddings and Major Events:**

1. **Trial Run** (Recommended) - We'll test styles and find your perfect look. $40 for trial session.

2. **Day-of Styling** - I arrive at your location 15 minutes early to set up. Allow 45-60 minutes per person.

3. **Final Touches** - Photos, adjustments, and touch-ups to ensure perfection.

4. **Touch-Up Kit** - I'll leave you with a small kit for emergencies.

### Before Your Appointment

**For Best Results:**
- Come with clean, dry hair (second-day hair often holds styles better)
- Bring any hair accessories, veils, or headpieces you plan to wear
- Wear a button-up or loose top that won't disturb your style
- Have inspiration photos ready (Pinterest is great for this!)

**Hair Preparation:**
- Don't wash hair the morning of your event (slightly dirty hair holds better)
- Avoid heavy products for 2 days before
- Don't get significant color changes within a week of your event

### Day of Your Event

I arrive fully equipped:
- Professional styling tools and products
- Emergency kit (bobby pins, hairspray, etc.)
- Portable lighting for precision
- Music upon request
- Patience and attention to detail
