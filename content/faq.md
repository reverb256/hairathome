## Service & Booking

**What areas in Winnipeg do you serve?**
We proudly serve all areas within Winnipeg, including Central, South, West, and North Winnipeg. Our mobile salon travels throughout the city limits, so you can enjoy professional hair services wherever you are in Winnipeg.

**How long does a mobile hair appointment take?**
Appointment length varies depending on the service. A basic haircut typically takes 30-45 minutes, while color services may take 1.5-3 hours. Special occasion styling usually requires 1-2 hours. We'll provide an estimated time when scheduling your appointment.

**Do I need to provide anything for the service?**
Our mobile salon is fully equipped with all necessary tools, equipment, and products. You just need to provide an electrical outlet and adequate lighting. For longer services, having access to water for rinsing is helpful but not required for most services.

**Is the service sanitary?**
Absolutely! We maintain the highest standards of hygiene. All tools are thoroughly sanitized between clients using hospital-grade disinfectants. We follow all health and safety protocols as required by the province of Manitoba.

**What if I need to reschedule or cancel my appointment?**
We require 24 hours' notice for any rescheduling or cancellations. For appointments scheduled during peak times (Friday-Sunday), we recommend 48 hours' notice. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice may incur a fee.

## Pricing & Payment

**Are travel fees included?**
Yes, travel fees are included for all appointments within Winnipeg city limits. For services outside the city, additional travel fees may apply.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
We accept cash, e-transfer, and credit card payments through our mobile terminal. A 25% deposit may be required for first-time clients or services over $200.

**Do you offer packages or discounts?**
Yes! We offer bridal party packages, regular client discounts, and seasonal promotions. Contact us for current special offers.

## Safety & Quality

**Are you licensed and insured?**
Yes, I am a Red Seal Certified hairstylist with full professional liability insurance and all required Manitoba health and safety certifications.

**What products do you use?**
I use professional-grade products from leading brands including Kevin Murphy, Redken, and Pureology. All products are cruelty-free and selected for optimal performance and hair health.

**Can you work with all hair types?**
Absolutely! I have experience working with all hair types, textures, and conditions. During your consultation, we'll discuss your specific hair needs and choose the appropriate products and techniques.
