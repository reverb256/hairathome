## Key Color Trends Identified

### 1. **Dark Backgrounds with Metallic Accents**
- Most premium sites use deep blacks/dark grays
- Gold and rose gold are dominant accent colors
- High contrast for luxury feel

### 2. **Typography Hierarchy**
- Serif fonts for headings (Playfair Display style)
- Clean sans-serif for body text
- Elegant, readable combinations

### 3. **Accent Color Psychology**
- **Gold**: Luxury, premium, exclusive
- **Rose Gold**: Modern, warm, approachable
- **Platinum**: Cool, sophisticated, professional
- **Burgundy**: Rich, creative, dramatic

### 4. **Layout Patterns**
- Clean, minimalist designs
- Ample white space
- Strategic use of color for CTAs
- Professional photography with consistent styling

## Recommended "Svelte Hair Look" Colors

Based on the analysis, here are the top recommendations for a sophisticated dark theme:

### Primary Recommendation: "Luxury Midnight"
```css
--bg-primary: #0a0a0a;    /* Deep black */

--accent-gold: #d4af37;   /* Luxury gold */
--text-primary: #ffffff;  /* Pure white */
```
