# Beauty Industry Color Palette Recommendations for Hair@Home

## Analysis Summary
Based on visual analysis of leading beauty industry websites (Snapps.ai, Fresha, Booksy) and current Hair@Home design, the following color palette recommendations will enhance the site's alignment with beauty industry standards.

## Recommended Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Champagne Gold**: #f3e6d0 (backgrounds, elegant accents)
- **Rich Gold**: #d4af37 (luxury accents, highlights)
- **Warm Rose**: #d4a998 (subtle highlights, beauty accent)
- **Soft Coral**: #e8c4a8 (call-to-action elements)

### Text Colors
- **Rich Brown**: #3d2e26 (primary text)
- **Off-White**: #f9f5ee (light text on dark backgrounds)

### Accent Colors
- **Deep Espresso**: #1e1916 (dark backgrounds, luxury feel)
- **Soft Taupe**: #a89b8e (subtle borders, secondary elements)

## Implementation Guide

### For Headers
- Use Rich Gold (#d4af37) for primary headings
- Use Rich Brown (#3d2e26) for secondary text

### For Buttons
- Primary buttons: Gradient from Rich Gold to Soft Gold
- Secondary buttons: Rich Gold outline with transparent fill

### For Cards & Sections
- Background: Champagne Gold (#f3e6d0) with subtle gradients
- Text: Rich Brown (#3d2e26) for readability
- Accents: Rich Gold (#d4af37) for highlights

### For Images
- Apply subtle golden overlay for luxury feel
- Use soft frame effects in Rich Gold

## Benefits of This Palette
- Aligns with luxury beauty industry standards
- Improves readability and user experience
- Creates an upscale, premium feel
- Maintains good contrast for accessibility
- Evokes feelings of relaxation and luxury
