## 2. Color Psychology Deep Dive

### Primary Gold (#d4af37) - The Luxury Anchor

**Psychological Associations:**
- **Premium Quality**: Gold universally signals excellence and high value
- **Trust & Reliability**: Warm gold conveys dependability and expertise
- **Success & Achievement**: Associates brand with success and high standards
- **Timeless Elegance**: Classic luxury that doesn't fade with trends

**Beauty Industry Context:**
- Differentiates from clinical blues (Fresha) and bold reds (Redken)
- Positions service as premium, not budget-friendly
- Appeals to affluent mobile service customers
- Creates perception of high-end salon experience at home

### Champagne (#f3e6d0) - The Sophistication Base

**Psychological Associations:**
- **Elegance & Refinement**: Soft, sophisticated backdrop
- **Approachability**: Warmer than clinical white, more inviting
- **Cleanliness & Purity**: Light tone suggests hygiene and care
- **Premium Background**: Elevates other colors and content

**Beauty Industry Context:**
- More sophisticated than standard white backgrounds
- Creates warm, inviting atmosphere for beauty services
- Differentiates from cold, clinical competitors
- Supports luxury positioning without being intimidating

### Beauty Rose (#d4a998) - The Feminine Connection

**Psychological Associations:**
- **Beauty & Femininity**: Direct connection to beauty industry
- **Care & Nurturing**: Warm, caring emotional response
- **Modern Sophistication**: Contemporary take on traditional beauty
- **Personal Attention**: Suggests personalized, caring service

**Beauty Industry Context:**
- More unique than standard pinks used by competitors
- Appeals to target demographic (women 25-55)
- Creates emotional connection to beauty and self-care
- Supports personal, intimate mobile service model

### Coral Accent (#d49976) - The Modern Energy

**Psychological Associations:**
- **Creativity & Modernity**: Fresh, contemporary energy
- **Confidence & Boldness**: Modern, self-assured positioning
- **Warmth & Vitality**: Energetic, life-affirming qualities
- **Fashion-Forward**: Trendy without being fleeting

**Beauty Industry Context:**
- Differentiates from traditional beauty colors
- Appeals to fashion-conscious clients
- Creates visual interest and excitement
- Supports creative hair services and styling
