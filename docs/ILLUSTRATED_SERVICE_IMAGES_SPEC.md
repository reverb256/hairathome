# Illustrated Service Images Specification

## Brand Color Palette

**Light Mode Colors:**
- Cream: `#FBF8F3` (warm off-white background)
- Vanilla: `#F5EDE0` (soft beige)
- Sand: `#E8DCC6` (warm tan)
- Copper: `#A66B48` (primary accent - warm reddish-brown)
- Terracotta: `#A65D3F` (secondary accent - earthy red-brown)
- Honey: `#D4AF37` (gold/yellow accent)
- Rose: `#C4A99A` (soft pinkish-brown)
- Blush: `#E8C5B3` (warm pink)
- Amber: `#B0956C` (golden brown)

**Dark Mode Colors:**
- Espresso: `#2D2520` (dark brown background)
- Mocha: `#3A2F28` (medium-dark brown)
- Cocoa: `#4A3F38` (lighter brown)
- Gold: `#C9A86C` (primary accent - warm gold)
- Champagne: `#E5D4B3` (light beige-gold)

## Design Principles

1. **Warm & Earthy**: Use the brand's copper, terracotta, honey, gold color family
2. **Minimalist & Clean**: Flat design with simple geometric shapes
3. **Professional**: Elegant, not cartoonish or childish
4. **Subtle Gradients**: Soft color transitions matching the brand aesthetic
5. **Consistent Style**: All 6 images should share the same visual language
6. **Background**: Use cream/vanilla gradients for light mode compatibility

## Technical Specifications

**Common Settings:**
- Format: PNG with transparency (for versatility)
- Size: 1200x900px (4:3 aspect ratio to match current cards)
- Style: Flat vector illustration style
- Anti-aliasing: High quality
- Background: Transparent PNG OR subtle gradient from brand-cream to brand-vanilla

## Service-Specific Specifications

### 1. Haircuts

**Visual Concept:** Stylized scissors with flowing hair
- Primary element: Elegant, minimalist scissors (copper/gold outline)
- Secondary element: Soft, flowing hair strands (copper to honey gradient)
- Composition: Scissors in center, hair flowing around them
- Style: Clean lines, geometric but organic feel
- Colors:
  - Scissors: Copper outline (#A66B48), gold fill (#C9A86C)
  - Hair: Gradient from copper (#A66B48) to honey (#D4AF37) to amber (#B0956C)
  - Accent: Small circular scissors pivot point (gold #C9A86C)

**Mood:** Professional, precise, elegant

---

### 2. Color Services

**Visual Concept:** Abstract color spectrum with hair silhouette
- Primary element: Soft hair outline/profile (light copper, semi-transparent)
- Secondary element: Color spectrum/rainbow (subtle, muted)
- Composition: Side profile of hair with colors flowing through/around it
- Style: Organic shapes, painterly but controlled
- Colors:
  - Hair silhouette: Copper/rose gradient (#A66B48 to #C4A99A), 30% opacity
  - Color spectrum: Horizontal bands (honey #D4AF37, rose #C4A99A, blush #E8C5B3, amber #B0956C)
  - Background: Subtle vanilla to sand gradient (#F5EDE0 to #E8DCC6)

**Mood:** Artistic, creative, vibrant

---

### 3. Blowout & Styling

**Visual Concept:** Wind lines with voluminous hair shape
- Primary element: Stylized hair with volume (rounded, fluffy shape)
- Secondary element: Airflow/wind lines (swooshing curves)
- Composition: Hair shape center, wind lines radiating outward
- Style: Dynamic, movement-oriented, smooth curves
- Colors:
  - Hair shape: Soft gradient from blush (#E8C5B3) to champagne (#E5D4B3)
  - Wind lines: Semi-transparent copper (#A66B48, 40% opacity)
  - Motion particles: Small gold circles (#C9A86C, 60% opacity)
  - Accent: Honey highlights (#D4AF37)

**Mood:** Light, airy, movement, volume

---

### 4. Men's Cuts

**Visual Concept:** Geometric, angular shapes with clean lines
- Primary element: Stylized barber scissors or clippers (geometric)
- Secondary element: Sharp angular lines/shapes (square, triangle accents)
- Composition: Bold, symmetrical or strongly asymmetrical
- Style: Modern, masculine, minimalist, architectural
- Colors:
  - Main tools: Dark charcoal/espresso outlines (#2D2520)
  - Fill: Copper and terracotta (#A66B48, #A65D3F)
  - Accent lines: Gold (#C9A86C)
  - Background shapes: Mocha/cocoa (#3A2F28, #4A3F38) with low opacity

**Mood:** Sharp, professional, modern, clean

---

### 5. Updos & Formal

**Visual Concept:** Elegant curves with intertwined braiding patterns
- Primary element: Stylized updo/bun shape (elegant curves)
- Secondary element: Braiding patterns (intertwined lines)
- Composition: Centered bun/shape with decorative braiding around
- Style: Sophisticated, feminine, ornamental but not ornate
- Colors:
  - Hair shape: Rose to blush gradient (#C4A99A to #E8C5B3)
  - Braiding lines: Copper and gold alternating (#A66B48, #C9A86C)
  - Accent dots: Small champagne circles (#E5D4B3)
  - Background: Soft vanilla (#F5EDE0)

**Mood:** Elegant, sophisticated, graceful

---

### 6. Treatments

**Visual Concept:** Organic circles with nurturing patterns
- Primary element: Water droplets or soft circles (organic shapes)
- Secondary element: Botanical/accent elements (small leaves or dots)
- Composition: Scattered droplets/circles, gentle flow
- Style: Natural, caring, organic, soft
- Colors:
  - Droplets: Blue-teal tinted with brand colors (copper-blue, teal-green)
    - Actually: Stay in brand - use sand/champagne (#E8DCC6, #E5D4B3) with slight blue-green tint
  - Accents: Small amber/gold dots (#B0956C, #D4AF37)
  - Flow lines: Very subtle copper (#A66B48, 20% opacity)
  - Background: Cream to vanilla gradient (#FBF8F3 to #F5EDE0)

**Mood:** Nurturing, natural, calming, refreshing

## Unified Style Guidelines

**Line Quality:**
- Stroke width: 2-3px for main elements
- Stroke style: Smooth, slightly rounded caps
- No harsh angles unless intentional (Men's Cuts)

**Color Usage:**
- Main shapes: Copper, terracotta, honey, gold gradients
- Accents: Champagne, amber, rose, blush
- Outlines: Copper or charcoal (very dark)
- Always use brand colors - no outside palette

**Gradient Directions:**
- Generally: Diagonal (top-left to bottom-right) or radial
- Keep subtle - 30-50% opacity shifts
- Avoid harsh transitions

**Composition:**
- Center-focused for consistency
- Leave breathing room (20% padding)
- Element size: 60-70% of canvas
- All service images should feel balanced and stable

**Avoid:**
- Photo-realistic elements
- Cartoonish or childish style
- Neon or saturated colors
- Complex patterns that don't scale
- Text or numbers in the illustration
- People or faces (keep abstract/symbolic)

## Quality Checklist

Before finalizing each image:
- [ ] Uses only brand colors (copper, gold, honey, terracotta, etc.)
- [ ] Matches the warm, earthy aesthetic
- [ ] Clean, minimalist, professional appearance
- [ ] Scalable and readable at card size (400x300px view)
- [ ] Transparent background OR matches cream/vanilla gradient
- [ ] Consistent style with other service images
- [ ] Abstract/symbolic, not literal or cartoonish
- [ ] No text, numbers, or faces
- [ ] Professional and appropriate for a mobile hair salon
