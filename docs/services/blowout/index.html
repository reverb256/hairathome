Your Blowout Experience

What to Expect

When you book a blowout with Hair@Home:

  1. Consultation - Discuss the look you want and what’s achievable for your hair type
  2. Wash & Condition - Professional shampoo and conditioning treatment
  3. Prep - Heat protectant products applied
  4. Drying - Professional blowout technique for smooth, voluminous results
  5. Styling - Finishing touches, curls, or straightening as desired
  6. Setting - Products to help your style last

Before Your Appointment

  • Come with dirty hair (or I can wash it - either way works!)
  • Have inspiration photos ready if you have a specific look in mind
  • Ensure adequate lighting and access to an electrical outlet
  • Wear a button-up or loose top to avoid disturbing your style afterward

After Your Appointment

  • Your blowout should last 3-5 days with proper care
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz
  • Use a shower cap when bathing
  • Refresh with dry shampoo between washes

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a blowout take? Most blowouts take 30-45 minutes. Special occasion styling may take 45-60 minutes.

How long will my blowout last? With proper care, a professional blowout can last 3-5 days.

Do I need to wash my hair before? No! In fact, slightly dirty hair holds style better. Freshly washed hair is fine too.

Can you work with my hair extensions? Yes, I’m experienced with both tape-in and clip-in extensions.

What if I don’t like the result? Your satisfaction is important. I’ll work with you until you’re happy with the result.
