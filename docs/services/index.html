Our comprehensive range of mobile hair services brings the salon experience directly to you. Each service is performed with professional-grade products and equipment, ensuring exceptional results in the comfort of your own space.

Service Areas

We proudly serve all areas within Winnipeg, including:

  • Central Winnipeg (Downtown, Exchange District, Wolseley, Osborne Village)
  • South Winnipeg (Fort Garry, St. Vital, St. Boniface, Transcona)
  • West Winnipeg (Charleswood, St. James, Fort Richmond, Headingley)
  • North Winnipeg (Seven Oaks, River Heights, North Kildonan, St. Johns)

Booking Information

  • Travel Fee: Included for all Winnipeg city limits
  • Equipment: All tools and products provided
  • Sanitation: Hospital-grade sanitation protocols
  • Scheduling: Flexible including evenings and weekends
  • Same-day: Available based on availability

Cancellation Policy

We require 24 hours’ notice for cancellations. For appointments during peak times (Friday-Sunday), 48 hours’ notice is appreciated.
