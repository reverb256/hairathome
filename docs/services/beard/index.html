Your Men’s Cut Experience

What to Expect

When you book a men’s haircut with Hair@Home:

  1. Consultation - Discuss your preferred style, face shape, and maintenance routine
  2. Preparation - Cape application, hair sprayed and sectioned
  3. The Cut - Professional techniques using clippers and/or scissors
  4. Detail Work - Clean neckline, sideburns, and around ears
  5. Beard Work (if selected) - Trim, shape, or line-up
  6. Finishing - Styling products applied for a polished look

Before Your Appointment

  • Have an idea of the style you want (or I can suggest options)
  • Wear a button-up or loose shirt to avoid disturbing your look
  • Ensure adequate lighting in the area where we’ll set up
  • Come with clean, dry hair (or I can wash it for you)

After Your Appointment

  • Styling tips for maintaining your look at home
  • Product recommendations (no pressure - just helpful suggestions)
  • Information about when to schedule your next cut (typically 3-4 weeks)

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you bring everything? Yes! I bring all clippers, scissors, products, and equipment needed for a professional cut.

Should I wash my hair before? It’s best to come with clean, dry hair. However, I can wash it for you if needed.
