Your Color Service Experience

Before Your Color Appointment

  • Avoid washing hair for 24-48 hours before coloring (natural oils help protect your scalp)
  • No other chemical treatments for 2 weeks prior
  • Come with dry, unwashed hair
  • Wear a button-up shirt (avoid pulling clothes over your head after coloring)
  • Have photos of colors you like ready for consultation

Color Services Explained

Root Touch-Up ($45)

Perfect for maintaining your current color. I color just the regrowth area where your natural color shows through. Takes about 60-75 minutes.

Single Process Color ($55)

All-over color from roots to ends. Great for:

  • Covering gray hair
  • Changing your overall shade
  • Adding richness and depth Takes about 90 minutes.

Partial Highlights ($55)

Highlights placed on the top and sides of your head - the areas most visible. Creates dimension and brightness. Takes about 90-120 minutes.

Full Highlights ($65)

Highlights throughout your entire head. Maximum lift and dimension. Takes about 2-2.5 hours.

Balayage (Custom Pricing)

Hand-painted highlights for a natural, sun-kissed look. Less maintenance than traditional highlights. Pricing depends on hair length and desired effect.

Color Maintenance Tips

Between appointments, protect your investment:

  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Wash with lukewarm or cool water (hot water fades color faster)
  • Limit sun exposure or use UV-protectant products
  • Avoid chlorine when possible (or wet hair before swimming)
  • Schedule touch-ups every 4-6 weeks for optimal results

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does hair color take? Most color services take 90 minutes to 2.5 hours, depending on the service.

Do you offer men’s color? Yes! I offer gray blending, full color, and highlights for men’s hair.

Will the color damage my hair? Professional color is formulated to minimize damage. I also include conditioning treatments with every color service.

How often do I need to touch up my color? Root touch-ups are typically needed every 4-6 weeks. Highlights can go 8-12 weeks between appointments.

Do you carry all color products? I carry professional color for most applications. If your desired color requires specialty products, I’ll discuss options during consultation.
