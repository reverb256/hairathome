Frequently Asked Questions

Service & Booking

What areas in Winnipeg do you serve? I serve all areas within Winnipeg city limits. I come to your home with my mobile setup:

  • Downtown Winnipeg (Exchange District, The Forks, Osborne Village)
  • South Winnipeg (St. Boniface, St. Vital, Fort Garry)
  • West Winnipeg (Charleswood, St. James, Assiniboine Park)
  • North Winnipeg (Seven Oaks, River Heights, St. Johns)
  • East Winnipeg (Transcona, Elmwood)

How long does a mobile hair appointment take? Appointment length varies by service:

  • Basic haircut: 30-45 minutes
  • Haircut with wash: 45-60 minutes
  • Basic color (root touch-up): 1-1.5 hours
  • Simple event styling: 45-60 minutes

I’ll give you a time estimate when you book.

Do I need to provide anything for the service? You need to provide:

  • An electrical outlet (standard 120V)
  • A sink with running water (for wash services)
  • Adequate lighting
  • Enough space for my portable chair

I bring all tools, products, and equipment. For color services, you’ll need access to warm water for rinsing.

Is the service sanitary? Yes. I follow basic hygiene practices:

  • Tools are cleaned between clients
  • I use clean capes and towels for each appointment
  • I follow Manitoba health guidelines
  • Patch tests are done for new color clients

What if I need to reschedule or cancel my appointment? I require 24 hours’ notice for cancellations. For weekend appointments, 48 hours is appreciated. Last-minute cancellations (less than 24 hours) may incur a $20 fee. I understand emergencies happen - just contact me as soon as possible.

Can my pet be around during the appointment? For safety reasons, pets should be kept in another room during the service. If that’s not possible, let me know when booking and we can discuss options.

Pricing & Payment

Are travel fees included? Yes, travel is included within Winnipeg city limits. For areas outside the city (Headingley, Birds Hill, etc.), a $15 travel fee applies.

What payment methods do you accept?

  • Cash
  • E-transfer (preferred)
  • Credit/debit cards

A 25% deposit may be required for first-time clients or wedding bookings.

Do you offer discounts? Yes:

  • Family discount: 10% off when booking 3+ haircuts in one visit
  • Combined services: Save $5 when adding a beard trim to a haircut
  • Referral: $10 off for you and your referred friend

Do you accept gift certificates? Yes, I offer gift certificates for any amount. They never expire and can be used for any service.

How do you keep prices affordable?

  • No salon rent or overhead to cover
  • I focus on efficient, straightforward services
  • I use good quality products without the luxury markup
  • One-person operation means no staff costs to pass on

What I Can (and Can’t) Do

What color services do you offer? I keep color simple:

  • Yes: Root touch-ups, basic all-over color, simple highlights, gray coverage
  • No: Color corrections, fashion colors (vivid blues/pinks), bleach and tone, balayage, complex multi-dimensional color

As a mobile stylist, I carry essential color products only. For complex color work, I recommend visiting a full-service salon.

Do you do perms, relaxers, or keratin treatments? No. These chemical treatments require specialized equipment and ventilation that I can’t provide in a mobile setup. I can refer you to salons that offer these services.

Can you work with all hair types? I have experience with:

  • Fine, medium, and coarse hair
  • Straight, wavy, and curly hair
  • Basic styling for all textures

For intricate braiding, locs, or specialized textured hair services, I may refer you to a stylist who specializes in those techniques.

What products do you use? I use reliable, mid-range professional products that work well without the premium price. All products are standard professional salon brands - nothing fancy, just effective. If you have allergies or sensitivities, let me know and I’ll check ingredient lists.

Before Your Appointment

How do I prepare?

  • Haircuts: Come with clean, dry hair
  • Color: Don’t wash your hair the day of (natural oils help protect scalp)
  • Event styling: Bring reference photos and any accessories you want to use
  • General: Wear comfortable clothing that you don’t mind getting a bit of hair on

Do you offer consultations? Yes, I do a quick consultation before every service to understand what you want. For color services, I require a patch test 48 hours before if you’re a new client or trying a new color.

What if I’m not happy with the results? Let me know right away if something isn’t right. I’ll do my best to fix it. If I can’t meet your expectations, I won’t charge you. I want you to be satisfied with the service.

Safety & Health

What if I have allergies? Tell me about any allergies when booking. For color services, I do a patch test 48 hours before your first appointment to check for reactions.

Can you work with pregnant clients? Most services are safe during pregnancy, but please let me know and I can adjust products if needed. I avoid certain stronger chemicals for pregnant clients.

What about weather cancellations? Winnipeg winters can be rough! If weather makes travel unsafe, I’ll contact you to reschedule. I monitor the forecast and won’t risk safety for either of us.

Are you insured? Yes, I carry liability insurance for mobile services.
