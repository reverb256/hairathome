Proper Technique: Curly hair requires different cutting methods

  • Remove weight without sacrificing length

The Curly Girl Method

This service incorporates principles of the Curly Girl Method:

  • No harsh sulfates that strip natural oils
  • Condition-focused cleansing
  • Gentle drying techniques
  • Minimal heat exposure
  • Products that enhance, not suppress, natural texture

At-Home Curl Care

Wash Day Routine:

  1. Cleanse: Sulfate-free shampoo or co-wash
  2. Condition: Generous application, focused on ends
  3. Detangle: Wide-tooth comb, working from ends up
  4. Style: Apply products to dripping wet hair
  5. Dry: Air dry or diffuse on low heat
  6. Scrunch: Break cast when fully dry

Between Washes:

  • Refresh with water and conditioner mix
  • Pineapple technique for sleeping
  • Satin pillowcase to reduce frizz
  • No brushing dry curls!

Why Curls Need Special Care

Fragile Structure: Curly hair is more prone to breakage

Dryness Tendency: Natural oils don’t travel down the hair shaft easily

Frizz Factors: Humidity and handling cause frizz

Product Sensitivity: Wrong products can weigh down or dry out curls

Cut Matters: Regular cutting techniques don’t work for curls

Common Curl Challenges Solved

Frizz Control: Proper hydration and gentle handling

Flat Roots: Root clips and strategic cutting for lift

Stringy Ends: Regular trims and moisturizing products

Lack of Definition: Right products and application techniques

Triangle Shape: Layered cutting to remove bulk

Product Recommendations

Based on your curl type, I recommend:

Cleansing: Gentle, sulfate-free shampoo

Conditioning: Rich mask for weekly deep conditioning

Styling: Curl cream + lightweight gel combo

Maintenance: Silk pillowcase and microfiber towel
