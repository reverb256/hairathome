# Hair At Home - Winnipeg Mobile Hair Stylist

Welcome to the Hair At Home website repository! This is a professional one-page website for a mobile hair stylist serving Winnipeg, Manitoba.

## About

Hair At Home brings professional salon-quality hair services directly to your doorstep in Winnipeg. Our mobile stylist comes equipped with all the tools and products needed to deliver exceptional results in the comfort of your own home, office, or any location within Winnipeg.

## Features

- Fully responsive one-page design optimized for mobile devices
- Professional services showcase with pricing
- Online booking form
- Service area information specific to Winnipeg neighborhoods
- Photo gallery of hairstyles
- Client testimonials
- Frequently asked questions section
- Contact information and social media integration

## Services Offered

- Haircut & Style: Custom cuts and styling for all hair types and lengths ($35 - $70)
- Wash & Blowout: Premium wash, conditioning, and styling ($30 - $50)
- Color Services: Full color, highlights, and touch-ups ($150 - $300)
- Special Occasion: Updos, curls, and styling for weddings and events ($60 - $120)
- Treatments: Deep conditioning and scalp treatments ($40 - $80)
- Beard Grooming: Cuts, trims, and shaping for men ($25 - $50)

## Service Areas

We proudly serve all areas within Winnipeg, including:
- Central Winnipeg (Downtown, Exchange District, Wolseley, Osborne Village)

## How to Use

1. Navigate to the website to view services and information
2. Use the online booking form to request an appointment
3. Our team will contact you to confirm your appointment details
